Welcome to the OARTB

01 January 2020

The first public version, a real alpha-book, of the Open Augmented Reality Teaching Book is available.

The book is both for students learning about mixed reality and for teachers seeking inspiration for the design of courses.

The content is far from being complete. We have five different badges for signaling the maturity of content.
